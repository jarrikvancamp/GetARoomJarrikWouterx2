Info

The hotel rooms are bright and have air conditioning, coffee and tea facilities and a flat-screen TV. You can enjoy a free selection of new films and blockbusters in your room. For breakfast you can choose from a selection of hot and cold dishes.

The fitness centre offers a relaxing setting after a business meeting or a busy day of shopping. The bar serves Belgian beers and cocktails. The pentalounge provides a wide selection of international food and drinks.

pentahotel Brussels City Centre is a 5-minute car drive away from Brussels-South Train Station, which offers Thalys and Eurostar connections to international destinations. The surrounding area offers many restaurants and cafés. The Louise metro station is a 7-minute walk away.
